About the cottage

The cottage is an old fisherman's home with modern comforts. There's a sofa bed, kitchenette, toilet and a small deck.

  • Sofa bed: Pull out the lower part and fold down the backrest.
  • TV: Press the red button on the remote. Choose HDMI1 for streaming.

Within biking distance

  • Andréns bageri

    A bakery where barely anything has changed for the last 100 years, even the owners are the same family now as when they started. Bread and sweet treats, and children always get a free cinnamon bun with every purchase!

  • Uttorps naturreservat

    [Add details here]

  • Kvarnen

    Once an active windmill for making flour, now renovated to a restaurant and café. You can climb to the top for views — try local dishes like kroppkakor or take a pizza to bring home.

  • Skärgårdskraft

    Museum, second hand shopping, café and local crafts. Learn about Tjurkö's long history of stonecutting.

  • Bibbis skärgårdscafé

    Summer café serving classic Swedish treats and lighter foods — and even a wine made locally on Tjurkö.

Food to try / Mat att testa

  • Kalles kaviar med smör och knäckebröd

    Smoked cod roe in a tube — try it on crisp bread with butter.

  • Saltlakrits

    [Add details or favourite shops here]

  • Inlagd sill

    Pickled herring in many flavours — often eaten at holidays.

  • Kroppkakor

    Potato dumplings filled with pork and onion — served with melted butter, cream and lingonberry jam.

    Sponge cake with custard, jam and whipped cream under green marzipan.

    Open sandwich with mayo, egg and lots of fresh shrimp — often finished with lemon and dill.

    Pick-and-mix candy sold by weight — available across stores.
